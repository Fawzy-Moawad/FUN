    About Us

    We are a Canadian Community of Egyptians and EGYPT LOVERS in BC.
    The Egyptian Canadian Cultural Society of BC, ECCS-BC, is a dynamic non-profit organization gathering and serving all BC resident Egyptians and all BC’ían individuals and groups who have genuine interest in the Egyptian Mosaic Culture, Traditions, History and Future. We aim to connect, develop, support and promote interests of all our members and friends.

    The first General Assembly meeting was held on October 6, 1979, approving to name the newly born association then as “The Egyptian Canadian Society“, and was then registered in Victoria under number 15346.
    First Board members were: Ahmed Farid Fadl, Mahmoud Sobhy Khalil, Abdel Salam El-Gemeei, Ragheb Ashour (founding members), with Mahasen Hassan and Abdallah Sedky.

    On March 18, 1980, name changed to “The Nile-Fraser Egyptian Canadian Society of B.C.”, under registration number S-15346. And finally changed to our current name on March, 21st, 2011, and kept “Nile-Fraser” as the name of our newsletter.
    The Society started its Egyptian Arabic School in 1983, with the first class held on September 24, 1983, with 11 students, and was held in The Pakistan Center in Vancouver.

    First School Committee members then were the Pioneers: Ahmed El Neweihi, Mahmoud Sobhi Khalil, Zakaria El Ramly, Nagat El Essily and Abdel Magid Mousa.
    Now The Egyptian Arabic School is back in full service to all our Members and Friends, with its site in Burnaby, BC.

    OUR VISION

    To be the Hub Organization gathering and serving all BC resident Egyptians and other individuals and groups who have genuine interest in the Egyptian Mosaic Culture, Traditions, History, and Future.

    OUR MISSION

    We will reach out to all prospective members, and will innovatively develop sustainable means to connect, develop, support and promote interests of all members, honoring the Authentic Mosaic Traditions and Values of the Egyptian Culture and Society.

    OUR VALUES

    We respect diverse ideologies, religions, traditions and backgrounds.
    We honor voluntarism as our source of power, We believe in community service and service beyond self.
    We ensure transparency and integrity in all what we do.

    Soccer

    The Egyptian Canadian Cultural Society of BC (ECCS BC) is Proud to present the Egyptian Soccer Club, ESC, as part of its activities. EAS is all run by Volunteers. The Club is a place for all Egyptians and Arab residents of BC. Every Soccer lover can come join and enjoy, practice and mock games. All Ages from 14 and up are welcome. ECCS BC uses turf or grass fields where available, and indoors gym during fall and winter. Come and register with us and join the fun.
